Money washed based on knocking on door
</br>
**How it works**
- Knock on door
- Give the ped the money
- Wait for a message
- Collect the money
- Chance defined so they can steal the money

</br>
When collecting the money there will be a car with 4 peds
</br>
When knocking on the window, one ped will step out to give you the washed money/
</br>
If scammed the ped with the suitcase will run.
</br>
When killed you can take the money
<br>
Note that it is synced so anyone can take the money
